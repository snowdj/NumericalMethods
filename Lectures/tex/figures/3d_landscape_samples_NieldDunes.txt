Simulated vegetated aeolian dune landscapes under different
environmental conditions. Dune types range from stabilised parabolic
dune landscape (d) under ideal growth conditions, through to
transgressive transverse dunes (c) and mixed dunes (a) under drought
conditions. (Nield and Baas, 2008, Global and Planetary Change)
